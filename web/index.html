Our Challenge

Natural resources are the foundation of human well-being and economic opportunity. But today, we are depleting Earth’s resources at rates that are not sustainable, endangering economies and people’s lives. People depend on clean water, fertile land, healthy forests and a stable climate. Livable villages and clean energy are essential for a sustainable planet. At Viyomam, we aim to develop solutions to address these urgent, global challenges this decade.

Our Vision

We envision an equitable and prosperous planet by empowering the Farmer based on traditional knowledge and driven by the wise management of natural resources. We aspire to create a world where the actions of people are based on Compassion towards all living beings. Build thriving communities that sustain the natural environment and allow all life forms to live in Harmony.

Our Approach

We start with data. We conduct independent research and draw on the latest technology to develop new insights and recommendations.We use our research to test projects to build a strong evidence base. Then, we work with partners to deliver change on the ground that alleviates poverty and strengthens society.Once tested, we work with partners to adopt and expand our efforts to carry out our ideas and elevate our impact.
